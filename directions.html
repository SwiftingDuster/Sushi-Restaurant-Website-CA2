Directions Guide


Visiting us? Follow this quick and easy guide and you will be here enjoying the best sushi in no time!

Whether you are visiting by car/train or bus, we've got you covered! Click the buttons for detailed direction guide.


Mode of transport

Travelling by car:

(This is probably the simplest way of getting here!)

Senario 1: If you're already in Sushi Town

1. Drive here by taking the main road leading to Grove Street 86.

2. Next, drive north towards Benda Road and turn right into Acadia Bay.

3. Finally, drive straight for about 2 minutes and you should see the Sushi Restaurant's sign! (It's hard to miss!).

Senario 2: If you're from another City

1. Get onto the Adam Highway and exit at Exit 0

2. Drive straight for 10 minutes and you should see the Sushi Town's main attraction. It's a giant Sushi in the middle of the town! If you see it, turn right!

3. Drive here by taking the main road leading to Grove Street 86.

4. Next, drive north towards Benda Road and turn right into Acadia Bay.

5. Finally, drive straight for about 2 minutes and you should see the Sushi Restaurant's sign! (It's hard to miss!).

Travelling by Train:

(For those who wants to save the world from pollution)

Senario 1: If you're already in Sushi Town

1. Get to the North-South(NS) Line from your location and alight at NS10 Woodlands Station.

2. Take Bus 300 from the Bus interchange right under the train station. Alight 3 stops later at Acadia Bay.

3. Walk north for 10 minutes and you should see the Sushi Restaurant's sign! (It's hard to miss!).

Travelling by Bus:

(Old but gold)

Senario 1: If you're already in Sushi Town

1. Get to the Woodlands Bus Interchange from your location.

2. Take Bus 300 from the Bus interchange right under the train station. Alight 3 stops later at Acadia Bay.

3. Walk north for 10 minutes and you should see the Sushi Restaurant's sign! (It's hard to miss!).
